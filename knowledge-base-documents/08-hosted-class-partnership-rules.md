# Hosted Class and Partnership Intake Rules

Source type: issue-specific intake rule
Audience: Athena ticket-intake assistant

## Definition

Hosted classes and partnership experiences are specialized sessions curated with influencers, wellness partners, community builders, brands, or partner instructors who bring their audience into the Physique 57 Method.

## Strategic goals

Capture intelligence for:
- Prospect quality
- Partner audience alignment
- Conversion potential
- Guest follow-up
- Brand visibility
- Content and social amplification
- Partnership quality
- Revenue pipeline impact

## Structured fields to prefer

Use select for hostedFeedbackArea:
- Guest experience
- Partner quality
- Prospect quality
- Conversion opportunity
- Event operations
- Instructor feedback
- Social/content opportunity
- Follow-up needed

Use select for prospectQuality:
- High Fit
- Moderate Fit
- Low Fit
- Existing Members Mostly
- Unable to Determine

Use select for followUpPreference:
- Phone Call
- Email
- WhatsApp
- Instagram DM
- In-Person Next Visit
- No Follow-up Needed
- Member Will Reach Out

Use number for:
- Guest count
- New prospect count
- Existing member count
- Follow-up lead count

## Good follow-up questions

Ask:
- What was the partner or host name?
- What type of audience attended?
- Did guests express interest in continuing practice?
- What follow-up channel did attendees prefer?
- Were there operational issues during the event?

Avoid:
- Asking whether the event was "successful" as a subjective rating.
- Asking for generic feedback when specific lead quality or follow-up signals are needed.
